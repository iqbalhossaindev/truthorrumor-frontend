About TruthOrRumor

Built to challenge misinformation with evidence and clarity.

TruthOrRumor is a professional news and fact checking platform built to challenge misinformation, misleading claims, and online rumors. In a time when information spreads quickly and confusion spreads even faster, we focus on evidence, clarity, and responsibility.

Our mission is to verify trending news, investigate viral claims, and publish reliable reports that are easy to understand and trustworthy. We review sources carefully, compare facts responsibly, and help readers understand what is true, what is misleading, and what remains unverified.

We cover breaking news, political claims, viral social media content, and major world events. We are not here to add more noise to the internet. We are here to reduce confusion, bring facts to the surface, and keep truth in focus.

TruthOrRumor is designed for real readers. The platform includes country filters, topic filters, truth or rumor filters, accessibility tools, and a clean reading experience that helps people stay focused on verified information without distraction.

At our core, we believe trust must be earned. Accuracy comes before speed. Evidence comes before opinion. Responsibility comes before attention. Every report we publish follows one principle: readers deserve information they can believe in.

What Makes Us Different

Built for truth, designed for every reader.

TruthOrRumor is designed not only to verify information, but also to make reading clearer, more comfortable, and more accessible. Alongside fact checking and readable reporting, the platform includes practical tools that help readers adjust how they read and interact with content.

Our accessibility approach is built around clarity, control, and inclusion. Readers can personalize visual comfort, improve readability, and use support features that make verified information easier to reach, understand, and trust.

Blind Support

TruthOrRumor includes a dedicated Blind Support feature that can be turned on or off with a triple tap anywhere on the page, making the feature fast and easy to access.

Voice Support

Voice based reading support helps readers listen to content more easily, creating another way to follow verified information without relying only on on screen reading.

Screen Reader + Focus Navigation

Live announcements and focus navigation support improve reading flow for assistive use. When Blind Support is on, readers can move through readable content with Arrow Up, Arrow Down, J, and K.

Reading Controls

Readers can personalize visual comfort through theme controls, font size adjustment, spacing controls, and cleaner reading layouts designed for longer and more comfortable sessions.

Haptics, Auto Scroll, and Alerts

Across the website, readers can also use haptic feedback, auto scroll, and notification based updates to create a smoother and more personalized reading experience.

Braille Inspired Haptic Experiment

On supported devices, readers can experience a Braille inspired haptic experiment as an additional accessibility layer alongside voice, screen reader support, and focus navigation. This is a haptic accessibility experiment, not a physical Braille display.
