About TruthOrRumor

TruthOrRumor is a professional news and fact checking platform dedicated to helping people separate real facts from false claims, misleading stories, and online rumors.

In today’s fast moving digital world, information spreads quickly, but not all of it is true. Our mission is to verify trending news, investigate viral claims, and present clear, reliable, and easy to understand reports that people can trust.

At TruthOrRumor, we focus on accuracy, transparency, and responsibility. We review sources carefully, compare facts, and highlight what is true, what is misleading, and what is only rumor.

What We Do

We publish clear reports, summaries, and deep analysis to help readers understand breaking stories, political claims, viral social media content, and world events.

Our platform is designed to make reading easier with filters, saved stories, accessibility options, and a clean experience that helps people focus on verified information.
