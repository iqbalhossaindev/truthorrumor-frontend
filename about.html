About TruthOrRumor

Built to challenge misinformation with evidence, clarity, and responsibility.

TruthOrRumor is a professional news and fact checking platform built to challenge misinformation, misleading claims, and online rumors. In a time when information spreads quickly and confusion spreads even faster, we focus on evidence, clarity, and responsibility.

Our mission is to verify trending news, investigate viral claims, and publish reliable reports that are easy to understand and trustworthy. We review sources carefully, compare facts responsibly, and help readers understand what is true, what is misleading, and what remains unverified.

We cover breaking news, political claims, viral social media content, and major world events. We are not here to add more noise to the internet. We are here to reduce confusion, bring facts to the surface, and keep truth in focus.

TruthOrRumor is designed for real readers. The platform currently operates in two editions: a Bangla edition for local and regional readers at www.truthorrumor.com/bd, and an English Global edition for wider international coverage at www.truthorrumor.com. This structure helps present verified information in both regional and global contexts while creating a strong foundation for future expansion.

At our core, we believe trust must be earned. Accuracy comes before speed. Evidence comes before opinion. Responsibility comes before attention. Every report we publish follows one principle: readers deserve information they can believe in.

TruthOrRumor is not here to follow the noise. It is here to challenge it, verify it, and reveal the truth.
Our Mission

Helping people read with confidence.

Our mission is simple and clear: to help people read with confidence. We work to verify important stories, challenge questionable claims, and present information in a way that is responsible, transparent, and easy to follow.

The platform is designed to make verified information easier to understand through country filters, topic filters, truth or rumor filters, accessibility tools, and a clean reading experience without distraction.

What We Cover

Breaking news, political claims, viral social media content, online rumors, major world events, and truth focused reports.

How We Verify

We identify the original claim, review available sources, compare evidence carefully, and publish a clear conclusion for readers.

How We Verify

A clear process for responsible reporting.

  • We identify the original claim and the source behind it.
  • We review available evidence, official statements, and credible reporting.
  • We compare facts carefully across sources before drawing conclusions.
  • We separate confirmed information from misleading or unverified claims.
  • We publish a clear, readable conclusion so readers can understand the outcome quickly.
